Windspeed Versus Latutide




Analysis

There appears to be very little correlation between windspeed and distance to the equator. All latitudes had a wide range and high variance of observed windspeeds.

The highest recorded windspeeds were in Norway where they were experiencing a small storm at the time. It was labeled as a "Light intensity shower."
